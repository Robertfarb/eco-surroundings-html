EcoSurroundings represents sustainable and environmentally-friendly materials and surfaces to architects, designers and the building community in Michigan and Ohio.
Our materials are also used for countless types of products, including: furniture, boats, knives, landscaping features and even guitars! We partner with our clients to achieve their goals while fostering better ways to care for our planet.

 

 

 

 

About Me
I believe in representing products that are responsibly-produced to foster a regenerative world. My product lines are carefully curated to evoke a greater consciousness about the things by which we surround ourselves.

 

Susan Hall Bio:

Since 2016, Susan has been representing sustainable building materials in Michigan. Prior to founding EcoSuroundings, she worked for Cranbrook Academy of Art and Art Museum, a leading educational and art institution. Susan previously served as executive director of The Blight Authority, a nationally acclaimed Detroit-based nonprofit focused on blight removal. Susan has also held multiple account management roles in advertising and service enterprises. She earned a bachelor of arts in social relations and policy from James Madison College at Michigan State University. She is a member of NAWIC and CAM.
